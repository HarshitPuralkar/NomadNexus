Paris Itinerary

5 Days in Paris

Day 1: Arrival & Eiffel Tower

Arrive in Paris and check-in at your hotel. Start your trip with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower, followed by a relaxing stroll along the Seine River.

Hotels: Hotel Eiffel Seine, Mercure Paris Centre Eiffel Tower

Day 2: Louvre & Notre-Dame

Explore the world-renowned Louvre Museum in the morning. In the afternoon, visit the magnificent Notre-Dame Cathedral and its surroundings.

Hotels: Hôtel Regina Louvre, Hotel de Seine

Day 3: Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur

Spend the day in Montmartre, the artistic heart of Paris. Climb up to the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for breathtaking views of the city.

Hotels: Terrass" Hotel, Hôtel des Arts Montmartre

Day 4: Versailles Palace

Take a day trip to the stunning Palace of Versailles, exploring its opulent interiors and expansive gardens.

Hotels: Return to your hotel in Paris after the visit.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy your last morning in Paris with some last-minute shopping or a coffee at a local café before heading to the airport for your departure.

Hotels: Departure - Check out from your hotel.

5 Days in Paris

Day 1: Arrival & Eiffel Tower

Arrive in Paris and check-in at your hotel. Start your trip with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower.

Lunch: Le Jules Verne

Hotels: Hotel Eiffel Seine

Day 2: Louvre & Notre-Dame

Explore the world-renowned Louvre Museum in the morning. In the afternoon, visit the magnificent Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Lunch: Café Marly

Hotels: Hôtel Regina Louvre

Day 3: Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur

Spend the day in Montmartre, the artistic heart of Paris. Climb up to the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for breathtaking views of the city.

Lunch: Le Relais Gascon

Hotels: Terrass" Hotel

Day 4: Versailles Palace

Take a day trip to the stunning Palace of Versailles, exploring its opulent interiors and expansive gardens.

Lunch: Angelina Versailles

Hotels: Return to Paris.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy your last morning in Paris with some last-minute shopping before heading to the airport for departure.

Lunch: Café de Flore

7 Days in Tokyo

Day 1: Arrival & Shibuya Crossing

Arrive in Tokyo, check-in at your hotel, and visit the famous Shibuya Crossing.

Lunch: Uobei Sushi

Hotels: Shibuya Excel Hotel Tokyu

Day 2: Asakusa & Senso-ji Temple

Visit the historic Asakusa district and see Tokyo's oldest temple, Senso-ji.

Lunch: Tempura Kondo

Day 3: Akihabara & Tokyo Skytree

Explore Akihabara's vibrant tech and anime shops, then head to the Tokyo Skytree for stunning city views.

Lunch: Ramen Street in Tokyo Station

Day 4: Day trip to Mount Fuji

Take a day trip to Mount Fuji for scenic hikes and breathtaking views.

Lunch: Local bento on the way

Day 5: Meiji Shrine & Harajuku

Visit Meiji Shrine in the morning, then explore the trendy Harajuku area.

Lunch: Kawaii Monster Café

Day 6: Odaiba & TeamLab Borderless

Spend the day in the futuristic Odaiba district, ending with the immersive art experience at TeamLab Borderless.

Lunch: Decks Tokyo Beach

Day 7: Departure

Relax at your hotel or take a last stroll around Tokyo before your flight.

Lunch: Narita Airport restaurants

5 Days in New York City

Day 1: Central Park & Times Square

Spend the morning at Central Park, then head to Times Square in the evening.

Lunch: The Loeb Boathouse

Hotels: The Plaza Hotel

Day 2: Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island

Take a ferry to the Statue of Liberty and explore Ellis Island's history.

Lunch: Liberty Island Café

Day 3: The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Spend the day exploring the Met, one of the world's largest and finest art museums.

Lunch: The Met Roof Garden Café

Day 4: Broadway Show & Rockefeller Center

Watch a Broadway show and visit Rockefeller Center for stunning city views.

Lunch: John's Pizzeria

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy your last day in NYC before departing. Do some last-minute shopping on Fifth Avenue.

Lunch: Bryant Park Grill

6 Days in Bali

Day 1: Arrival & Ubud Monkey Forest

Arrive in Bali and visit the lush Ubud Monkey Forest.

Lunch: Bebek Bengil

Hotels: Four Seasons Resort Bali

Day 2: Tegallalang Rice Terraces & Uluwatu Temple

Explore the famous rice terraces and visit Uluwatu Temple for a sunset view.

Lunch: Café Pomegranate

Day 3: Tanah Lot Temple

Visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, perched on a rock by the sea.

Lunch: Warung Subak

Day 4: Nusa Dua Beach

Relax on the white sands of Nusa Dua Beach and enjoy water activities.

Lunch: The Pirates Bay Bali

Day 5: Kuta & Legian

Visit the vibrant Kuta and Legian areas for shopping and nightlife.

Lunch: Hard Rock Café Bali

Day 6: Departure

Relax at your hotel or take a final walk on the beach before departure.

Lunch: Café Lotus Ubud

3 Days in Jaipur

Day 1: Arrival & City Palace

Arrive in Jaipur and explore the City Palace, followed by a visit to Jantar Mantar.

Lunch: Samode Haveli (for royal Rajasthani cuisine)

Hotels: Rambagh Palace, Jai Mahal Palace

Day 2: Amber Fort & Hawa Mahal

Visit the majestic Amber Fort, followed by the famous Hawa Mahal in the afternoon.

Lunch: The Verandah at Rambagh Palace

Day 3: Jal Mahal & Departure

Enjoy the serene view of Jal Mahal in the morning, followed by some shopping at the local bazaars before departure.

Lunch: Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar (LMB, for traditional sweets and snacks)
